our idea

Every season, we'd find ourselves going back to the same stores buying new clothes that eventually made it to the back of our closets. Most of them were the "just-not-me-anymore" or "past-the-return-date" clothes. Eventually, we realized we could fuel our clothes-buying habit by offering our current clothes to other people to wear for short times. After we got many offers and started making bank, we couldn't keep it to ourselves. Now, renters and shoppers can easily connect to make easy money and find stylish clothes for events, vacations, and more. And when you're done, just give it back! Welcome to clothes done right.

so what are you waiting for...

Create an account and wait for the right occasion. Before going out to shop, check out theClothesLine, save some money, and help reuse.
